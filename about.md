I am a current York University Honours Physics student, conducting research in the field of experimental atomic physics. My research group makes use of home built external cavity diode lasers to perform experiments in precision metrology using a Magneto-Optical Trap. My work has included constructing a feedback system to stabilize the light intensity of our diode lasers using an analog circuit and acousto-optic modulator (AOM) to correct drifts in the intensity of these lasers. Presently, I am constructing an AOM modulated laser lock, to stabilize the output frequency of our cavity diode lasers to lock onto Rubidium resonances that are suited for experiments in the lab. My work has been presented at the 2024 Canadian Undergraduate Physics Conference and awarded amongst the Top 10 Best Research Presentations, the 2024 York NSERC USRA conference and can be presented in future publications from our group.
